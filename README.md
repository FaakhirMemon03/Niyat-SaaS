# NIYAT | Trust Infrastructure Layer

**"Building the Reliability Layer of the Internet"**

## Vision
Internet behavior ko understand nahi karta. Sirf activity ko record karta hai. Niyat is building the missing **Reliability Layer** of the internet. We are moving from a "Portfolio-based" identity to a "Behavioral-based" trust infrastructure.

## Core Thesis
Human behavior has patterns. AI, at enough scale, can predict reliability, trustworthiness, collaboration success, future conflict, and abandonment risk.

---

## 🚀 The Multi-Model Behavioral AI Architecture

Niyat uses a specialized ensemble of AI models to decode human trust:

1. **Identity Resolution Model**: Links identities across platforms (GitHub, Upwork, WhatsApp, Gmail) to build a unified behavioral profile.
2. **Communication Intelligence Model**: Analyzes tone, sentiment, and emotional stability to detect aggression or ghosting signals.
3. **Reliability Prediction Model**: Predicts deadline adherence, project abandonment, and payment stability.
4. **Behavioral Embedding Model**: Converts human interactions into multi-dimensional vectors for compatibility matching.
5. **Ghosting Detection Model**: Early warning system for disappearing behavior based on communication decay patterns.
6. **Trust Graph Engine**: A Neo4j-powered network mapping of who trusts whom and the quality of those links.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Architecture

### Data Ingestion Layer
- **Integrations**: Slack, WhatsApp, Gmail, GitHub, Jira, Payoneer, Stripe.
- **Event Collector**: Captures raw behavioral events (late payments, message delays, revision counts).

### Intelligence Layer
- **Behavioral Parser**: AI that understands the *meaning* behind events (e.g., is a delayed reply "busy" or "ghosting"?).
- **Temporal Memory Engine**: Tracks behavior evolution over time (e.g., increasing stress or declining consistency).

### Infrastructure
- **Backend**: Go / Node.js (Event-driven).
- **Databases**: 
  - **PostgreSQL**: Structured business data.
  - **Neo4j**: Relationship intelligence & Trust Graph.
  - **Qdrant/Pinecone**: Vector embeddings for behavior.
- **Streaming**: Kafka / RabbitMQ for real-time risk processing.
